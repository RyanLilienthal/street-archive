Walter Israel, born on November 9, 1925, lived with his parents, Theodor and Mina Israel, in Elmshausen, Germany--the family's home for generations. On March 18, 1942, Gestapo officers arrested the Israels, who were never heard from again. Posts on this website recall Walter and his parents through archival material and creative expression.
